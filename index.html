Chiagozie Uchenwoke

I am a forward-thinking architectural designer with a strong eye for detail and a commitment to innovation. My work blends creativity with practicality, delivering designs that enhance everyday living while reflecting unique client visions.

about me

I am a trained architect with a degree in Architecture from Federal University of Technology Owerri(F.U.T.O). Over the past 4years, I have gained valuable experience working on a wide range of projects, including residential, commercial, and mixed-use developments. My expertise lies in blending creative design concepts with practical solutions to deliver functional, sustainable, and visually striking spaces.

4 bedroom bungalow

The bungalow features a spacious living room, dining area, fully fitted kitchen with store, and four well-sized bedrooms (including a master en-suite). It includes a guest toilet, front veranda, and provisions for cross ventilation and natural lighting. The layout balances comfort and privacy while maximizing functionality on a single level.

6 bedroom duplex

The duplex offers six spacious bedrooms, including a master suite with walk-in closet and private bath. The ground floor accommodates a large living room, dining area, fully fitted kitchen with store, guest toilet, and one en-suite guest room. The upper floor houses additional bedrooms, a family lounge, and balconies for relaxation. The design emphasizes modern aesthetics, natural lighting, ventilation, and functional flow, providing both comfort and privacy for the family.

7 bedroom duplex

The duplex is designed to provide luxury and functionality, with three bedrooms on the ground floor and four bedrooms on the upper floor. The ground floor also features a spacious living room, dining area, kitchen with store, guest toilet, and a welcoming entrance porch. The upper floor accommodates the family lounge, master suite, additional bedrooms, and balconies for outdoor relaxation. The layout ensures privacy, smooth circulation, and abundant natural lighting and ventilation, combining modern aesthetics with comfort for a large family.

Water factory

A water bottling factory is planned with four main zones: Production area housing water treatment, bottling, and packaging lines; Storage area for raw materials and finished products with direct loading access; Administration section including offices, reception, and meeting spaces; and Staff quarters providing on-site accommodation with basic facilities. The layout ensures hygienic processing, smooth workflow, and efficient distribution.
